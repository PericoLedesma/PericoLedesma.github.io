Over the years, logical research on riddles has proven to be a fascinating topic. Theories and concepts can be put to the test by applying them to real-world problems. The riddle of 100 prisoners and one light bulb has been solved numerous times and is well known to the general public. The riddle was even extended with several additional puzzles by [1]. For this research we will modify the 100 prisoners and one light bulb puzzle into a riddle with more light bulbs and see how we can extend the existing protocols and create protocols for new solving strategies.
